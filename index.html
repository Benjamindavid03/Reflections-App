Privacy Policy

Reflections

Version 1.0.0

1. Information We Collect

  • User Data: We may collect personal data such as usernames, encrypted passwords, email addresses, profile pictures, and other data you provide when creating a user profile.
  • Content Data: The app collects information about the posts, comments, and media you share, including images, videos, and location (optional), to display it to your followers and other users.
  • Device Information: Technical information about your device, including IP address, device type, and operating system, may be collected to enhance app performance, security, and compatibility.
  • Interaction Data: We collect data regarding your interactions with other users, such as likes, comments, follows, and private messages, to facilitate the social networking experience.

2. How We Use the Information

  • To Provide and Improve Services: We use the information you provide to maintain and improve Reflections, including user interaction features, post sharing, and secure messaging.
  • Security: We utilize encryption techniques to protect your sensitive data, including passwords and private messages, ensuring that your data remains secure within the app.
  • Personalization: The app may use your preferences and activity data to personalize the content, posts, and user suggestions you see, including recommending new users to follow based on your interactions.

3. Data Sharing

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to legal proceedings, we may share your personal information to comply with legal obligations or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.
  • User-Generated Content: Content you share publicly within the app, such as posts, likes, and comments, may be visible to other users.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: We encrypt sensitive data, such as passwords and private messages, to prevent unauthorized access.
  • App Protections: Screens are protected against screenshots and screen recordings, further ensuring that your sensitive data is secure while using the app.
  • Limited Access: We restrict access to your data to authorized personnel only.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, update, and delete the personal data you have provided to Reflections. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the app's support feature. Additionally, you can control the visibility of your posts and interactions through the privacy settings in your account.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies for tracking within the app. However, we may use local storage for authentication and session management, ensuring a smoother user experience. Third-party services integrated with the app may collect data as per their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide you with the services of Reflections. If you choose to delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our system within a reasonable time, unless otherwise required by law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on this page. Your continued use of Reflections after any modifications signifies your acceptance of the new policy.
